# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in the
Docker Commander community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless
of background or identity. We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute
to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our standard

This project adopts the **[Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/), version 2.1**.
Please read the full text — including the examples of acceptable and
unacceptable behaviour and the enforcement guidelines — here:

<https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/>

In short: be respectful, assume good faith, and keep discussions constructive.
Conduct the Covenant lists as unacceptable — harassment of any kind included — is
not tolerated in any project space (issues, pull requests, discussions, commit
messages, and any other community channel).

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces and also when an
individual is officially representing the project in public spaces.

## Enforcement

Report concerns privately to the maintainer at **filip@koduj.dev**. All reports
are reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly, and the reporter's privacy is
respected. Maintainers will take any action they deem appropriate, up to and
including a temporary or permanent ban from the project, and are obligated to
keep the details of any report confidential.
